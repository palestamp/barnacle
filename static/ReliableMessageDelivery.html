ReliableMessageDelivery

7th January 2019 at 6:30pm
Policy

Complience of QueueType to this policy means that reliable message delivery is guaranteed by queue.

There a a few ways to guarantee aforementioned property, look through this page for more information on topic.

Acknowlegment

In case client processes message succesfuly, he must send ACK call to the broker. Acknowlegment marks delivery as successful and deletes (retire) message from queue.
